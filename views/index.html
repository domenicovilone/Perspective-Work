Works On Perspective

Works On Perspective is an idea for an online and physical book about the current pandemic, COVID-19. The goal is to gather people’s perspectives on an unprecedented situation. Anyone can contribute by answering questions in a Google Form made by us. The book will be accessible online for free on our website. Physical copies will be available on demand.

More information is available when viewing the questionnaire.

To the people that will participate, we want to thank you in advance. You will be the reason this book is possible.

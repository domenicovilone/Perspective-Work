Works On Perspective

Works On Perspective is an idea for an online and physical book focusing on the current COVID-19 pandemic. The goal is to gather people’s perspectives, ideas and thoughts on the ongoing pandemic and its effects. Anyone is welcome to contribute to the making of this book.
